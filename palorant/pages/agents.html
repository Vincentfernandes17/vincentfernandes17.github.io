AGENTS

Palorant's Agents bring unique abilities that shape the battlefield, each falling into one of four roles. Duelists, Initiators, Controllers, and Sentinels. Master their abilities, refine your strategy, and dominate every fight.

Duelists – Aggressive fighters who thrive in one-on-one combat. With mobility and damage-focused abilities, they lead the charge, securing kills and creating space for their team.

Controllers – Battlefield tacticians who shape engagements by manipulating vision and space. Using smokes, barriers, and area-denial tools, they dictate the pace of combat and support team strategy.

Sentinels – Defensive specialists focused on securing key areas and protecting teammates. With traps, healing, and surveillance tools, they excel at holding positions and shutting down enemy advances.

Initiators – Tactical disruptors who break enemy defenses and set up engagements. Their abilities reveal enemies, apply crowd control, or force opponents out of position to give their team the advantage.
