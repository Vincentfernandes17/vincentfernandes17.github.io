Welcome to 2025, Palorant players! A new year means fresh beginnings, bold plays, and of course—new firepower. What better way to start the year than with a revamped, high-tech upgrade to one of the most iconic skin lines in the game?

Get ready to RE:FUEL your arsenal because the RE:FUEL // ARAXYS bundle is dropping in the shop on January 7th! Let’s dive into what makes this skin line a must-have.

Araxys Reloaded: A Deadly Sci-Fi Makeover

The Araxys skin line has always been a favorite for players who love alien technology-inspired weaponry with a sleek, metallic design. Now, RE:FUEL // ARAXYS takes it to the next level, offering enhanced visuals, upgraded animations, and new sound effects that make every shot feel more powerful than ever.

What’s in the RE:FUEL // ARAXYS Bundle?

This premium bundle brings a full arsenal of weapons redesigned with a sleek, futuristic aesthetic, featuring:

Prepare to wield some of the most advanced, stylish weapons in the game—because when Araxys is on the battlefield, enemies know they’re up against something out of this world.
