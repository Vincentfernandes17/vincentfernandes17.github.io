Palorant Patch Notes 1.00

Vapor joins the lineup, along with fresh cosmetics, a revamped map rotation, improved Remake Voting mechanics, and additional updates.

Patch Notes | 20/1/2025

Palorant is shaking things up once again! This time, we’re welcoming Vapor, a mysterious new Agent ready to change the game. But that’s not all—there’s a revamped map rotation, fresh cosmetics, and improved Remake Voting mechanics to keep the action flowing. Let’s dive into everything this update has in store!

Meet Vapor: The Latest Addition to Palorant’s Roster

Every new Agent brings a fresh way to play, and Vapor is no exception. While details on their abilities are still under wraps, early teasers suggest a highly mobile and deceptive playstyle, perfect for outmaneuvering enemies. Whether you love setting up sneaky plays or dodging bullets like a pro, Vapor is shaping up to be a game-changer. Get ready to experiment with new strategies and redefine the meta!

New Cosmetics: Drip or Drop

A Palorant update wouldn’t be complete without some fresh skins and cosmetics. This patch brings stylish new weapon skins, sprays, and player cards to add some extra flair to your loadout. Whether you’re looking for sleek and futuristic designs or something bold and flashy, there’s something for every player. Time to flex on your opponents in style!

Revamped Map Rotation: Fresh Battlegrounds Await

If you’ve been waiting for a map shake-up, this update delivers. The map rotation is getting an overhaul, meaning some old favorites might be taking a break while fresh battlegrounds enter the spotlight. This keeps matches feeling dynamic and forces players to adapt to new sightlines, strategies, and engagements. Whether you love mastering new maps or just want a break from the usual, this update will keep things exciting.

Improved Remake Voting: A Better Experience for Everyone

We’ve all been there—starting a match only to find yourself down a teammate before the game even begins. With the updated Remake Voting system, it’s now easier and more reliable to call for a remake when things go south early on. This ensures fairer matches and a smoother experience, so no more frustrating 4v5 starts!

Final Thoughts: Big Changes, Big Plays

This update is shaping up to be one of the most exciting yet. With Vapor entering the scene, new cosmetics to collect, a revamped map pool, and quality-of-life improvements, there’s plenty to look forward to. Whether you’re here to master the new Agent, explore different battlegrounds, or just freshen up your look, Palorant continues to evolve in all the right ways. So, what are you most excited about in this update? Let us know in the comments and see you in the queue!
